Tobacco use and mortality project involves the analysis of 5 datasets containing information about the drugs, prescriptions, death rates, hospital admission rates etc

The Objective is:

1. To analyze the deaths due to tobacco consumption based on the given 5 datasets
  Admissions.csv
  Prescriptions.csv
  Fatalities.csv
  Metrics.csv
  Smokers.csv
2. To integrate the datasets and gather insights
3. To find relationship between deaths and the factors causing it
